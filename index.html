About Our School

Dedicated to providing high-quality education that nurtures the academic, social, and personal growth of every student.

Established in 1998, Manav Bharti National School is a co-educational, English medium day school recognized by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. Our school offers a robust curriculum designed to empower students at the secondary level and prepare them for future success.

Our campus, located in Kendui near Govt. ITI on Bodhgaya Road, Gaya, boasts a spacious, well-designed building with a secure high boundary wall. We are committed to creating an inspiring and supportive atmosphere where students are motivated to achieve their full potential. With a strong focus on academic excellence, discipline, and holistic growth, our school remains a pillar of quality education in the community.

Admission Procedure & Guidelines

Manav Bharti National School welcomes student inquiries and requests from families seeking high standards of learning, community, and personal responsibility.

01

Inquiry & Prospectus

Visit the administrative block near Govt. I.T.I, Bodh-Gaya Road between 08:00 AM and 02:00 PM to collect registration forms.

02

Document Submission

Submit filled forms along with birth certificates, transfer certificates (TC), and academic transcripts of the prior grade.

03

Assessment & Interview

A basic grade-level skill interaction with teachers to map academic compatibility and developmental targets.
